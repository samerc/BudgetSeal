# BudgetSeal Beta — Tester Guide

Thanks for helping test BudgetSeal! Here's everything you need to know.

---

## What is BudgetSeal?

A budgeting app that uses the "envelope" method — you assign every dollar a job. Income goes into your account, you fund envelopes (Groceries, Rent, etc.), and spending draws from those envelopes. You always know where your money is.

The app is 100% offline, no account needed, no data leaves your device.

1. I'll send you a link to join the Google Play internal test
2. Open the link on your Android phone
3. Tap "Accept invite" then install from the Play Store
4. The app appears as "BudgetSeal" with a blue envelope + wax seal icon

## Getting Started (2 minutes)

1. Open the app — you'll see the onboarding screen
2. Enter a household name (e.g., "My Budget" or your name)
3. Pick your currency
4. Create your first account (e.g., "Cash" or "Bank Account")
5. Tap "Create & Start"

## What to Test

Please try these flows in order. Spend about 15-20 minutes total.

### Round 1 — Core flow (5 min)
- Add an income transaction (tap + on the Activity tab)
- Go to Budget tab → tap an envelope → tap Fund → enter an amount
- Add an expense transaction and assign it to a category
- Check that the Dashboard shows your income, expense, and envelope balances

### Round 2 — Explore features (5 min)
- Try the Assisted transaction entry (long-press the + button → pick a type)
- Create a recurring transaction (More → Recurring & Bills → +)
- Try different themes (Settings → Appearance → Theme → try Dark and Black)
- Switch language if you speak Arabic or French (Settings → Appearance → Language)

### Round 3 — Stress test (5 min)
- Add a few more transactions quickly
- Try editing a transaction (tap it → tap the pencil icon)
- Try deleting a transaction (long-press → Delete) — you'll get 5 seconds to undo
- Go to Reports tab and check if the charts look correct
- Try the search on the Activity tab

### Round 4 — Edge cases (optional, for thorough testers)
- Try entering a very large amount (e.g., 999,999,999)
- Try entering special characters in notes (emojis, Arabic text, symbols)
- Try rotating your phone while on different screens
- Try using the back button rapidly
- Kill the app and reopen — does your data persist?
- Try the Web Companion (More → Web Companion → set a PIN → Start → scan QR from laptop browser)

## What to Look For

- Crashes (app closes unexpectedly)
- Screens that freeze or show infinite loading
- Text that looks wrong (cut off, overlapping, wrong language)
- Buttons that don't respond when tapped
- Numbers that look wrong (wrong currency symbol, wrong decimal places)
- Anything that feels confusing or unintuitive
- Dark mode issues (text you can't read, colors that look wrong)

---

## How to Report Issues

Send me a message with:
1. **What you did** — "I tapped X on the Y screen"
2. **What happened** — "The app crashed" / "The number showed wrong"
3. **Screenshot** — if possible, take a screenshot before/after
4. **Device info** — your phone model and Android version (Settings → About Phone)

Even small things matter — "this text feels too small" or "I didn't understand what this button does" is useful feedback.
