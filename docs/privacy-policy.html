Privacy Policy

Your Data Stays on Your Device

BudgetSeal is designed with your privacy as a core principle. Your financial data belongs to you — we never collect, store, or transmit it to any server. The app works 100% offline with no account required.

No Data Collection

BudgetSeal does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising libraries, or tracking pixels. No usage data, device identifiers, or behavioral metrics are collected. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your data.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable Cloud Sync, your data is uploaded to your personal Google Drive account or a file storage provider you select (Dropbox, OneDrive, or local storage). BudgetSeal does not have access to your Google account credentials — authentication is handled by Google's OAuth system.

You may optionally encrypt your sync file with AES-256 encryption using a password you set. The password is stored only on your device in secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain). We never have access to your password or encryption keys.

Web Companion

The Web Companion feature runs a local HTTP server on your phone, accessible only from devices on the same WiFi network. No data is sent to the internet — the connection is entirely local between your phone and your browser. The server requires PIN authentication and stops automatically when the app is closed.

Biometric Authentication

If you enable biometric lock, authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android BiometricPrompt / iOS LocalAuthentication). BudgetSeal does not store or process biometric data.

Permissions

  • Internet — Required only for optional Cloud Sync and exchange rate fetching
  • Network/WiFi State — Used by the Web Companion to detect your local IP address
  • Notifications — For bill reminders and budget alerts (you control these in Settings)
  • Camera — Optional, for receipt scanning in the Bill Splitter feature
  • Biometric — Optional, for app lock screen

Third-Party Services

BudgetSeal connects to the following external services only when you explicitly use the related feature:

  • Google Drive API — For Cloud Sync (only if you enable it)
  • Exchange rate API — For fetching currency exchange rates
  • Google Fonts — For loading custom fonts

No data about your transactions, budget, or financial information is sent to any of these services.

Children's Privacy

BudgetSeal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
